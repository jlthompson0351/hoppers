📋 How to Calibrate

  1. Start empty - Remove all weight from the scale
  2. Add 0 lb point - Enter 0 in the field below and click "Add Point"
  3. Add known weights - Place a known weight on the scale, wait for STABLE, enter the weight and click "Add Point"
  4. Repeat - Add 3-5 points across your operating range for best accuracy
